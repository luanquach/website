START-UP SINGLE PAGE WEBSITE

A Single Page Website would make it a lot easier for your users to get your point and the information you would like to tell.

How Do a Single Page Website Benefit Businesses?

It’s Affordable

A beautiful one page site can be delivered in just one week and at a lowest price point. With pageless design we might actually see, for the first time, the democratization of beautiful and effective web design. Making it possible for anyone who wants one to have a website that feels right, looks great and gets results.

Uses Story to Compel Visitors to Action

You need to make good use of the available space by using words that are direct and specific. This will make it easier for your user to get your point and the information you are trying to convey them.

Navigation is Easy

Since there is only one page, your users will not get lost so you don’t need to worry about the navigation. Most probably you won’t need a navigation bar or if you want you can keep one navigation bar.

Focus is on Quality

You can simply focus on your website without thinking about the other pages of the site. You will have a better website with good quality and better results.

Better Rranking on Google

Since there is only single page, there are more chances for increased Google ranking. This means that the ranking will be applied to the whole site because there is a single page which can lead to higher traffic.

Simple Enough for a Start-up Business

It’s simple, straightforward design combined with a great story and visceral interactions propel site visitors along a single path towards that final goal.

Better User Experience

Your users will surely have a good work experience if you have a single page website because there will be not many pages to navigate. They will have an easy access to the information and they will not have any problem on how they can get into a relevant topic of your site.

It’s Viscerally and Emotionally Satisfying

With the advances in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript we are able to weave interactive elements throughout our story to encourage, delight and win over our visitors on a “gut level,” which mere fact listing could never accomplish.

They’re Unique

One page sites are not the norm in web design, giving them a unique flare. Don’t get me wrong, well-designed multi-page websites are pretty awesome too, but if a designer only has to focus on creating one page, there’s more room to get creative and think outside of a standard website setup.
