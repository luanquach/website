What is Augmented Reality (AR) ?

Augmented Reality (AR) is all about merging amazing digital content seamlessly into the real world around us. To trigger this content, users simply scan a trained image, package, 3D object or even scan around a physical GPS location. The smartphone or other computing device acts as a lens, instantly unlocking 3D models, animations, games, videos, music and much more.

Augmented Reality allows brands, agencies and developers to bring a whole new reality to life. When digital content is mixed with the real world, users can better experience, understand and engage with your products, media and more.

How Augmented Reality (AR) Benefits Business?

The Engagement

AR increases engagement and interaction and provides a richer user experience.

Mobile Markets Growing

AR is mobile and personal and, therefore, hugely accessible to a rapidly growing smartphone market.

Costless

AR is an inexpensive alternative to other media platforms as no specific media needs to be purchased.

Great Marketing Tools

AR deliver more value to advertisers by offering measurable AR print ads with direct links to purchase.

New Level

Brands have access to detailed analytics enabling them to truly understand their audience.

Better ROI

Research has shown that AR increases the perceived value of products and brands.
